# Traitor Hunt Game Overview

## Overview
Traitor Hunt is an engaging multiplayer game focused on strategy, deception, and teamwork. Players are divided into two teams: the Traitors and the Hunters. The Traitors must work secretly to undermine the Hunters and achieve their objectives, while the Hunters must deduce who among them is a Traitor and eliminate them.

## Gameplay Mechanics
- **Teams:** Players are randomly assigned to either the Traitor or Hunter team.
- **Objectives:** Traitors aim to complete specific missions without being discovered, while Hunters seek to uncover and eliminate Traitors.
- **Communication:** Players can communicate with their teammates but must be cautious of their words, as Traitors can manipulate discussions.

## Features
- **Dynamic Environments:** Various maps and settings that provide unique challenges.
- **Roles and Abilities:** Each character may have different abilities that can influence gameplay.
- **Victory Conditions:** The game ends when either the Traitors complete their missions or all Traitors are identified and eliminated.

## Conclusion
Traitor Hunt combines elements of trust, strategy, and social interaction, making each playthrough a unique experience. Gather your friends, create a strategy, and dive into the world of Traitor Hunt!
